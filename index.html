Age of Tanks Warriors: TD War

Age of Tanks Warriors: TD War is an action-packed tower defense battle game where you fight your opponent throughout different historical eras. Starting from the Stone Age and progressing all the way to modern military warfare, your mission is to strategically dominate the battlefield and upgrade your army to victory.

Game Overview

In this epic 1v1 strategy battle game, you must constantly evolve your forces while defending your base. From primitive sticks and stones to advanced tanks and artillery, each era brings stronger weapons and more powerful fortresses.

The goal is simple: defeat your opponent’s army and destroy their shelter before they destroy yours. However, as the ages advance, so does the difficulty. Each level introduces stronger defenses and more tactical challenges.

How to Play

Age of Tanks Warriors is a one-click battle game, making the combat system easy to understand. Simply deploy your troops by clicking the soldier icon located at the bottom right of the screen.

The challenge lies in managing your troop deployment wisely. You may not always have enough soldiers available to match your opponent’s forces immediately, so timing and strategic planning are crucial.

Upgrade and Evolve

Once you defeat your enemy, you unlock upgrades for your army and weapons. Strengthening your units allows you to develop more effective battle strategies and prepare for tougher enemies ahead.

As you progress through time, the battlefield transforms dramatically. What begins with stone caves and primitive tools eventually evolves into high-tech military warfare featuring tanks and artillery.
