Description

Top Top Tank is a tank battle with pumping and upgrading the tank and super weapons.

Instructions

The goal of the game is to improve and upgrade your tank, survive on the battlefield, eliminate the enemy's tank army.

  • Keyboard:
    • W A S D / ↑ ← ↓ → — forward/backward movement / right-left rotation
    • Esc — pause
  • Mouse:
    • Movement — aiming at the target / overview
    • Right button — shot of the main weapon
    • Left button — additional weapon shot

Quick tips

  • Upgrade early to survive longer waves.
  • Use main weapon for consistent damage; use additional weapon for bursts.
  • Rotate smartly—positioning matters as much as firepower.
